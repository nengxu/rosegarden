Mastering (under construction)

This chapter will eventually deal with the vagaries of putting audio and MIDI streams together and mixing them down into a finished .wav file.

I don't expect to get to this until last. In the meantime, I offer the following quick and dirty information:

You can master in one of two ways. One is to set up a dedicated mastering track and play everything while having Rosegarden record itself. You can use Rosegarden's internal MIDI and audio mixers to help make this job easier.

The more flexible way is to route Rosegarden's JACK outputs into something else, like JAMin or Ardour. JAMin looks like a promising tool here, but I haven't really explored it.
