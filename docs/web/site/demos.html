Demonstrations of Rosegarden-4

If you're based in the UK and want to find out more about Rosegarden-4 then we'd be more than happy to talk to you with a view to demonstrating some of the features in person. We provide this service for schools, businesses or individuals according to how much time we have and where you're based.

Please send us an email if you want to take advantage of this service or want to talk to us further about Rosegarden and making music under Linux.

We're usually out an about around the country at an expo during the year. You can come and talk to us there. The next one is the Linux User and Developer Expo in Birmingham, UK on 24th-26th June 2003.
