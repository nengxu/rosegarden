Press Release: Rosegarden Music Software

London, 23rd September 2002

The Rosegarden Music Software project would like to announce that it will be showing the latest version of its music software (Rosegarden-4) at the UK Linux Expo in Olympia 2, London, UK on the 9th-10th October 2002.

The Rosegarden project produces music software that runs on the Linux operating system. The Rosegarden team will be appearing at the Expo to provide demonstrations and talk about their work as well as giving attendees the chance to record their own music on the day and take away a CD produced with the Rosegarden-4 product.

Rosegarden-4 is a MIDI and digital audio capable sequencer and notation editor for Linux. It allows the user to record, edit and produce music on their PC. It is freely available under the terms of the GNU Public Licence. Rosegarden-4 can be used with any major Linux distribution.
